AQUA house

This is a 3-story house, owned by a model family. Men prefer Western modernity and women prefer Asian inspiration. The girl is a pop fan and the son is a fan of hip-hop culture. Therefore, the Design Story is the synthesis and distribution of these distinct personalities into each living space, so that each member has their own space in the common family house.

 

Type: model house Gross floor area: 180 m² Status: complete Year: 2020 – 2021
